Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Subscription Email Cleaner Chrome extension handles user data.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. The extension operates locally on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Email Data

The extension only processes email data when you are actively using the Gmail app. Once you close the active tab, any processed email data is discarded and not saved.

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage: To store user preferences locally.
  • Active Tab: To access the content of the active tab when you are using the Gmail app.
  • Scripting: To inject scripts into the Gmail app for processing subscription emails.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services to collect or process your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
